  • How to use the search function.

    You can search for card numbers, names, card types, clans, traits and keywords.

    Go to a specific clan's page to filter only for cards in that clan.

    The search will return all instances of the search term. Which means that the more you type, the more refined the search will be. But also that you usually only need to type the first few letters of your search to get relevent results.

    For advanced searches, add the following characters before your search term.

    "c." to search for members of a particular clan. Example "c.lion", or just "c.l".

    "t." to search for traits. Example "t.bushi".

    "k." to search for keywords. Example "k.covert". Or just type "k." for a full list of characters with keywords.

    "e." to search for specific expansions. Example "e.core", or "e.toa" for Tears of Amaterasu.

    "i." will search for all cards who can be bought with influence, or "i'3", will search for cards with an influence cost of 3. Search on the individual clan pages to refine your search within than clan.

    You can search for "character', "event", "attachment", "holding", "province", "stronghold" or "role".
