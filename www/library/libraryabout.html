About

    The information presented in this app about Legend of the Five Rings the Living Card Game, both literal and graphical, is copyrighted by Fantasy Flight Games. This app is not produced, endorsed, supported, or affiliated with Fantasy Flight Games.

    Legend of the Five Rings LCG is property of Fantasy Flight Games. You must have at least one copy of the core game in order to play the game. This app is designed to be a quick and easy reference and resource for the game and it's cards.

    The information, text and images displayed in this app were collected from a variety of sources across the internet.

    Special mentions go to the amazing fiveringsdb.com who generously allowed me to use his fonts for the clan icons. Cardgamedb.com, the L5R subreddit & Facebook groups, and of course to FFG themselves.
